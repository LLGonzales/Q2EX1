A Charming Waltz

Original Poem by Lloyd Gonzales

The birds fly high,
As the students below sigh.
The wind around them halts,
As they hear a charming waltz.

The day shall be grim
And they'll arrive dejected.
But the mesmerizing hymn
Made their hearts resurrected.

Their thoughts had been scattered,
Enough to form their decline.
But the music made their confidence flattered
And their faces shine.

As the tune fades,
Their appearance looking satisfied.
Their worries fail,
As they walk off pacified.
